subti 0.0.1

Language help with hotkeys

Subti is a global interface for text manipulation in MacOS and Windows. Supporting all major LLM models, it automates AI queries with hotkeys. Among other things, it can be used for:

  1. Passive and active language learning. Let subti provide translated live subtitles to your writing or give you lists of vocabularies. The principle of learning languages passively through subtitling is well-documented.
  2. Quick translations and copy-editing. Get the right word or phrase straight away without having to ask. Unlike regular dictionaries, LLM:s support whole phrases.
  3. Fetching synonyms and alternative phrasings. Unlike regular synonym dictionaries, LLM:s can provide entire alternative phrasings.
  4. Custom AI queries. Subti lets you assign hotkeys to your common queries that can speed up manual tasks, whatever these may be.
  5. Speech to LLM. Send an AI prompt quickly by pressing a hotkey and speaking into your computer's microphone.
